    uinnova Technology Inc. Appoints Aplitt as Exclusive Distributor

    Seattle, Wash., Jan. 25, 2017-- uinnova Technology Inc., the leading 3D visualization solution for data centers, today announces to appoint Aplitt S.A. (WSE: API), the exclusive distributor in Poland, Scandinavia and Baltic States, and the reseller in the rest of the European countries.

    Uinnova 3D visualization allows you to visualize the business and IT infrastructure in one integrated 3D platform, monitor your data center and identify issues in logical and physical layers easily. When integrated with existing monitoring systems, the solutions give you total data center transparency and control, reduce business downtime and operation costs.

    Uinnova’s revolutionary 3D visualization solutions have been implemented and proven in more than 250 data centers, including many F100 corporations. Aplitt’s NOC (Network Operation Center) is also using the solution to provide services to its customers. NOC staff can visualize and identify the issues with the integrated monitoring solution, thus reduce its MTTR (Mean Time To Repair) and downtime, preventing potential financial losses.
