    3D Visualization Platform For Datacenter Management

    Datacenter Visualizer (uDCV) is the data integration and visualization platform, which enables users to visualize the information about the datacenter facility, storage, networks, servers, operating systems,virtual machines and applications.

    uDCV allows users to create ONE integrated and holistic 3D view for all IT staff, this reduces or eliminates the information asymmetry among different departments.

    uinnova solutions have been deployed to many datacenters, among the customers are large banks, energy sectors, government agencies, F100 enterprieses and IDC with millions of of managed IT equipment.

  • F eatures

    Asset Visualization

    - Asset location,physical appearance, advance multi-criteria search
    - Large 3D models library of IT infrastructure and equipment available
    - Integrated asset, configuration and alert information

  • Environment Visualization

    - Datacenter campus, buildings, floors, rooms and cages 3D views
    - Landscape and drone style fly-over views

  • Cabling Visualization

    - Electrical wires and conduits
    - Patch panel and cabling
    - Port-to-Port connection path
    - HVAC Utility pipeline

  • Capacity Visualization

    - U-level and cabinet level space utilization
    - Cabinet power consumption
    - Weight bearing distribution

  • Monitoring Visualization

    - IT equipment performance and alerting
    - Datacenter room temperature and humidity
    - UPS/PDU/Air Conditioning electrical power

  • Presentation Visualization

    - Self-direct 3D animation
    - Embedded Microsoft PowerPoint presentation in 3D environment
