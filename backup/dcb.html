    Visualization Platform For DataCenter Management

    Based on mature 3D graphics technology with proprietary intellectual property rights, uDCV builds a complete visualization model of your entire data center environment. You can check out every detail of your data center from anywhere through web browser.

    Data-driven Flatform

    uDCV is a Data-driven visualization platform for datacenter management, combined with years of data center and IT systems management experience, provides various professional functions to support daily operations of data center. The product has been applied to many types of data centers, including large banking, energy, government, business, IDC and other large data centers running thousands of devices and infrastrutures.
  • F eatures

    Easy to Use

    • Operating uDCV is similar to Google map by dragging and clicking. Meanwhile, the system provides fully control of visualization. In configuration page, you can customize the look of menus, buttons, control panel, graph layer, 3D scene angle and effects, etc. For example, set the model loading approach, 3D operation sensitivity, initialize the camera position and point of view, the function button to show or hide, logo icon, power / space / load range color, the color of the alarm level, the alarm icon size to accommodate different habits of the users.

    Enterprice Ready

    • Multiple-site data center model to adapt to unified management needs.
    • Multi-tenant management model, IDC and other data center service providers, can divide datacenter areas and group managed devices based on the actual tenants, through flexible access control, to grant proper access permissions to different users.
    • Simple and convenient online service to create datacenter visualization model. With the online service, you can quickly create the internal structure of the datacenter and fill in your managed devices (eg, cabinets, air conditioning, power distribution cabinets, generators, power supplies, mainframe, etc.), accurately reflect the status of every level of your datacenter.
  • D esign
    uDCV insists "simple and reliable" design principle, to provide users with a wealth of practical features, while maintaining the core system simplicity and robustness to meet consecutive 7 * 24 operation.
