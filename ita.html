    IT Architecture Visualization

    IT Architecture Visualizer (uITA) is the data consolidation and management platform. It enables real time IT architecture and component visualization, covering business transactions, applications, virtual machines, system, networks, storage and other IT infrastructure.

    uITA provides a unified information platform by integrating siloed operational data from CMDB, NMS, SMS, APM and ITSM,. This allows the enterprise to effectively manage its IT systems, assets, configuration and visualize the end to end architecture.
